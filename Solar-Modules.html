Solar Modules: The Right Solution for Any Site

Vispra solar modules are manufactured to the strict criteria of Vispra quality management. All products that bear the Vispra name stand for the highest quality. Our solar modules are manufactured under the SunTechnics brand, ranging from 5Wp to 230Wp. They meet the highest quality standards with compact design and high power output, making them perfect solutions for all SPV systems.
