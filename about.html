ABOUT US

Since 2010, Vispra Power Automation has been committed to providing innovative and reliable power protection solutions worldwide. Our products meet the highest standards of safety and quality, ensuring the continuity and protection of electrical devices.

We strive to create an environment of individual excellence and commitment to quality. Our comprehensive range of products includes standalone small UPS units, large systems, and everything in between, offering off-line, line-interactive, and on-line solutions tailored to various needs.
