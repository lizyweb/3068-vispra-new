Vispra Power Controls

Since its inception in 2010, Vispra Power Automation has been at the forefront of delivering top-notch power protection products globally. Our offerings are designed to meet international standards for safety and reliability, earning us worldwide recognition for quality, business integrity, and innovative engineering skills.

Vispra is dedicated to creating an environment that encourages individual excellence and a personal commitment to quality. With our ISO9001:2000 certification from the very beginning, we ensure that every product we develop, manufacture, and service maintains the highest standards. From small standalone UPS units to large-scale systems, Vispra provides comprehensive solutions to protect and ensure the continuity of electrical devices across various sectors.

ABOUT US

Since 2010, Vispra Power Automation has been committed to providing innovative and reliable power protection solutions worldwide. Our products meet the highest standards of safety and quality, ensuring the continuity and protection of electrical devices.

We strive to create an environment of individual excellence and commitment to quality. Our comprehensive range of products includes standalone small UPS units, large systems, and everything in between, offering off-line, line-interactive, and on-line solutions tailored to various needs.
